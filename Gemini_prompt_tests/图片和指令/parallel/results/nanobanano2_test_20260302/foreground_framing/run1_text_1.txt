This composition adds significant depth by utilizing natural foreground elements found in the winter scene. I have lowered the camera to place snow-dusted, dry grass and frosted twigs in the immediate foreground, forming a natural, out-of-focus dark frame. The familiar subjects—the lone bare tree and the rearing horseman—remain centered in the distance against the sunset horizon, framed perfectly by the blurred foreground texture, creating a much more layered and immersive photograph.
